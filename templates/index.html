What is AquaGuardians?

AquaGuardians is a website designed to address the issue of accidental drowning of children in migrant families in Australia. Important water safety information and resources are provided to help migrant parents make safe decisions when planning beach or other water activities. The goal of the webpage is to raise awareness of water safety and preventative preparedness, thereby reducing the incidence of drowning and ensuring that migrant families are able to safely enjoy Australia's rich water resources.
